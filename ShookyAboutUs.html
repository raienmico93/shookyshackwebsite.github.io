About the Owner: Babsy Glorioso Viray

Babsy Glorioso Viray is a proud K-pop fangirl who turned her passion into a business. As a big fan of K-pop and their character, she created Shooky Schock, a shop that is K-pop-inspired and sells Filipino foods. Through her business, Babsy shares her love for K-pop and connects with fellow fans in a fun and creative way..
